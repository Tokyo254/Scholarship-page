Scholarship Application (ICSS)

Established in 2021, The Integrated Centre for Sustainable Systems (ICSS) is a non-profit company leveraging on research, technology, development and capacity building to develop and extend management tools and strategies for developing sustainable integrated systems across agriculture, education, environment and health sectors.
The Centre has taken a smart sector-integration approach to help communities in Africa and across the world identify and solve the challenges that they are facing in these sectors; making the SDGs more cost-effective, efficient and sustainable in the long term.

Mission

Our main goal is to build integrated sustainable systems across the sectors of agriculture, education, environment and health.

Vision

To become a champion in developing integrated sustainable systems that solve the challenges facing the agriculture, environment, education and health sectors globally.
